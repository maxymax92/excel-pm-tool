## Use

- Vendor screenshots inform interaction and component roles.
- Excel examples inform construction, spacing and conditional-format techniques.
- The workbook design system determines final color, typography, geometry and accessibility.
- A source page is rechecked when a direct asset moves or a product interface changes.
